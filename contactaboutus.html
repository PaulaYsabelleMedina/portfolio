Vision

We believe that awe-inspiring creations are around us and within us. We envision a community that is inspired by science, excited to ask questions, and dedicated to improving the world around them through scientific research and innovation. We connect visitors to the research happening in their own community, and frames local technology and scientific advancements within the larger contexts of science, history and culture.
